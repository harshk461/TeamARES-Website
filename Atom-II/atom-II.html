ATOM — 2.0

Our Final Submission Rover for European Rover Challenge is the Atom 2.0, loaded with numerous refinements on the traversal pattern and movement boosts. Atom 2.0 is a testament to our team’s vision of creating the most intricate, and exciting Rovers.

Description

Atom 2.0, Packed with numerous upgrades, is an amalgamation of all our team’s tireless hard work and dedication towards providing the best. The Rover features an Auger Based Drill, Low-Pressure Balloon Wheels for the surface of Mars, an intricately solid suspension system, and a more refined Electronics setup using a high-performance Nvidia Jetson.

Glory

Qualified for the Final Round of Indian Rover Challenge in Chennai, and finished 13th. Prepared to participate in the European Rover Challenge Finals, to be held in September.
